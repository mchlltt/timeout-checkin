About Need Finder

Life can get crazy. Just turn on the news and see what we mean. Sometimes we just need to exhale, self-assess and take some time away. This app does just that. It's a simple way to take some much needed minutes out of our day to reflect, check our emotional state and take steps towards being in a better state of mind.


This app was designed by three coding ninjas - Chris, Mich and Matt - trained at the NU DOJO
